## Common Questions

**Q: Is my chat history private?**  
A: Chat history is stored only in your current session. It's cleared when you refresh the page.

**Q: Can the chatbot access my personal data?**  
A: No! The chatbot only answers general questions about CreditUp features and financial concepts.

**Q: What if the chatbot doesn't understand my question?**  
A: It will politely let you know and suggest related topics you can ask about.

**Q: Does the chatbot need internet?**  
A: No external APIs are used. Everything works with your local backend.

**Q: Is it available on mobile?**  
A: Yes! The chatbot works perfectly on phones, tablets, and desktops.
